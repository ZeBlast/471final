Dataset note

This page loads College Scorecard institution-level data and field-of-study data from CSV files in data/college_scorecard_data/ (most recent institution extract). Median earnings use only after-completion measures: 1_yr_after_completion.median → MD_EARN_WNE_1YR; 4_yrs_after_completion.median → MD_EARN_WNE_4YR. The map uses institutions that have coordinates; Decide search runs over the full parsed institution list.

Section 2

Explore: College Outcomes Map

Inspect colleges from the Scorecard extract. Color can emphasize earnings or debt, while point size reveals scale in cost or salary.

Zoom in, dig deeper: Now that you know which states produce the best outcomes overall, it is time to look at individual schools. Each dot on the map is a college. Color and size encode the metrics you choose. Densely populated regions like the Northeast can be hard to read at full zoom, so use the + / - controls or scroll to zoom, and drag to pan. Filter by state to isolate a specific region.

💡 Tip: Hover over any college dot to see its full profile in the panel on the right. Click a college dot to add it to your comparison list in Section 3 below, where you can directly compare debt burden against earnings trajectories side by side.
