Dataset note

This page uses institution-level PSEO rows filtered to inst_level = I, cip_level = A, degree_level = 05, then combined from the local CSV files in this folder. The cohort selector includes the aggregate 0000 rows plus every usable graduation cohort in the file. Availability of year 1, year 5, and year 10 earnings depends on the selected cohort.
