Dataset

This page uses College Scorecard institution-level fields from data/college_scorecard_data/Most-Recent-Cohorts-Institution.csv (same extract as Explore). Earnings use the same fields as Explore: MD_EARN_WNE_P6, MD_EARN_WNE_P8, and MD_EARN_WNE_P10 (median earnings 6, 8, and 10 years after entering college). Debt uses GRAD_DEBT_MDN (median debt for completers). Tuition on the horizontal axis uses TUITIONFEE_IN only (in-state published tuition), matching the Explore map’s “In-state tuition” metric. Admission rate uses ADM_RATE.

Section 1

Earnings vs. Price, Debt, or Selectivity

Compare median earnings (vertical) to admission rate, graduate debt, or in-state tuition (horizontal). Colors distinguish Public, Private, Prestigious private nonprofits, and For-profit colleges. Earnings horizons use the same P6 / P8 / P10 fields as Explore. Use the slider, category checkboxes, state filter, and horizontal-axis menu together.

Read the chart: With admission rate on the x-axis, further right means a larger share admitted. For debt and tuition, further right means higher sticker debt or published tuition. Prestigious schools are inferred from Scorecard fields; jitter reduces overplotting.

When you are ready to inspect individual campuses, open Explore. To compare specific schools side by side, use Decide on the Explore page.
