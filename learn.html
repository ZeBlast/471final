What do graduates earn after college?

Each dot is a campus with reported median earnings after completion. Color encodes who owns the school—public, private non-profit, private for-profit, or a selective slice of private non-profit we label “Prestigious” from Scorecard signals. Use the controls to change what sits on the horizontal axis and which earnings horizon is on the vertical scale.

Source: U.S. Department of Education College Scorecard, most-recent institution file. Same extract as Explore.

Median earnings are after completion only—one year and four years out—drawn from the same fields used on the map. Graduate debt uses median debt for completers. Tuition modes use published in-state or out-of-state tuition; admission rate is the share admitted.
